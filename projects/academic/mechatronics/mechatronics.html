Mechatronics Course Project

August 2025 - December 2025

3D Printing Prototyping Fabrication Integration Laser Cutting Team Collaboration

Project Overview

The Mechatronics course followed a project that the students vote on and create. This semester, our team chose to design and fabricate a autonomous vehicle to race Mario Kart style against other teams' vehicles. The competition involved an optional task of developing a dice pickup and sorting system.

Within our team of seven, we divided responsibilites into four subsystems: chassis and drivetrain, dice handling, electronics and control, and software and navigation. My primary contributions were within the chassis and drivetrain subsystem, where another team member and I designed and fabricated the vehicle chassis, selected motors and drivetrain components, and aided with integrating the electronics package, and dice handling system.

During the final competition, our vehicle successfully navigated the course autonomously, completing laps while avoiding obstacles, however we were unable to complete the dice pickup task. Regardless, our vehicle chinched 1st place overall due to its consistent navigation.

My Contributions

  • Brainstormed with team to design general concepts of the autonomous vehicle and its subsystems.
  • Iterated on design and prototyped full chassis with a laser cut acrylic baseplate, and 3D printed motor hub mounts.
    • Initial designs were to incorporate a belt-driven drivetrain, to save room for dice handling systems.
    • Final design utilized four individual motor drives for improved control and reliability.
  • Supported integration of electronics package and dice handling system.
  • Provided team with technical report template and aided with writing sections of the final report.

Results

  • Successfully designed and manufactured a laser cut acrylic chassis.
  • Vehicle consistently navigated the competition course autonomously, including ramps, bridges, and the gravel pit.
  • Final robot configuration demonstrated high reliability and consistency, earning the team 1st place overall in the competition.
